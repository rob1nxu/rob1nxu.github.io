Bagels @ The Affogato Bar, Singapore

The cafe had a pretty nice ambience and seemed like a good place to get a coffee and do some work from on a weekday. When we arrived, the cafe was generally quiet, only about 2-3 tables were occupied.


They have hand rolled bagels which got us really excited!
